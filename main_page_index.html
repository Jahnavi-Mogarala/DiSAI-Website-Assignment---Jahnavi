Overview of DiSAI

Digital Security Association of India(DiSAI) is a non-profit NGO registered as a public trust to create awareness on digital security. DiSAI was originally started as the fourth initiative of the monthly e-magazine ‘PreSense’ published by Prime Point Foundation, the other three being Sansad Ratna Awards (jointly with IIT Madras) for honoring outstanding MPs, Education Loan Task Force (for creating awareness among students and parents on education loans) and Digital Journalists Association of India (focusing on digital journalism), which also includes Next Generation Political Leaders (NGPL), for grooming young political aspirants. DiSAI has since become an independent organisation its own, out of the PrimePoint Foundation.

About DiSAI

DiSAI, founded on 17 November 2017 and registered as a public charitable trust in Chennai, is dedicated to creating public awareness about IT laws, cyber crimes, digital security, and data privacy. Through workshops, seminars, and lectures featuring judges, IAS/IPS officers, legal experts, and industry leaders, DiSAI educates diverse audiences on the importance of digital safety.

With the growing relevance of cybersecurity and legal awareness in today’s tech-driven world, DiSAI aims to build a digitally literate society by engaging students, professionals, and the public in understanding the risks and protections in cyberspace. It began its journey with an inaugural workshop at IIT Madras and continues to expand through student chapters and inclusive educational initiatives.

Vision

To strive ceaselessly in creating a digitally secure society in India. To empower citizens with knowledge of cyber laws, digital ethics, and online safety. To foster collaboration among legal experts, technologists, and policymakers for a resilient cyber ecosystem.

Mission

To create awareness in the society on the various components of digital security. To educate individuals about safe online practices, data protection, and cyber hygiene. To encourage proactive participation in building a secure and responsible digital environment.
